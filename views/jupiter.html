Jupiter

  • Jupiter Facts
  • The fourth brightest object in the Solar System after Venus
  • Babylonians first discovered Jupiter
  • Has the shortest day of all planets
  • Days last approximately 9 hours and 55 minutes
  • Orbits the sun once every 11.8 Earth years
  • Has very unique cloud features
  • The Great Red Spot is a huge storm on Jupiter
  • Made of rock, metal and hydrogen
  • Has the largest moon (Ganymede)
  • Has a thin ring system
  • Is significantly larger than the earth.
